3. Specific Requirements

    This system will be used for two major purposes: 1) to track the institution's lending programs, in terms of loans issued and loan repayments, and money mobilized in the form of subscriptions or deposits and 2) to track the institution's incoming sources of funds, in the form of grants, loans, investments, etc., and the subsequent allocation amongst lending programs, infrastructure, recurring costs, etc.
