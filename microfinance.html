What is microfinance?

Microfinance Defined
Microfinance programmes extend small loans to very poor people for self-employment projects that generate income, allowing them to care for themselves and their families. Within that broad definition, there are many types of microfinance. Find out more about them in this discussion from the Grameen Bank - Bangladesh.
