Mo-Ap Home Page

The Mo-Ap Project is conceived as an open source effort to develop standards, tools, libraries and solutions for the international micro-finance and micro-credit communities. We are just getting started, please click on the links below to read our documentation and subscribe to our mailing list to find out how you can get involved.

  • Open Source Architecture Document - a document prepared by James Dailey and Lynn McKiernan describing the case for automating Micro-finance with an Open Architecture
  • Mahakalasm MIS Functional Study - a detailed functional study of a SHG-Based Microfinance Organization in Southern India, documented by Vijay Pratap Singh and Tapan S. Parikh. By adding other functional studies, this can be used as the basis of our initial technical requirements and data format specifications
